Itinerary

Upon arrival at Veer Savarkar International Airport in Port Blair, you will be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel. After check-in, spend the rest of the day at leisure, exploring the local markets or relaxing at the hotel. Overnight stay at the hotel in Port Blair.

After breakfast, embark on a city tour of Port Blair, visiting the Cellular Jail, a historic prison that played a significant role in India's freedom struggle. Later, visit the Anthropological Museum, which showcases the rich cultural heritage of the Andaman Islands. In the evening, enjoy a light and sound show at the Cellular Jail, followed by an overnight stay at the hotel in Port Blair.

After breakfast, take a ferry to Havelock Island. Upon arrival, check in to your resort and relax. Later, visit Radhanagar Beach, renowned for its stunning sunsets and clear waters. Enjoy swimming or simply unwind on the beach. Overnight stay at the resort in Havelock.

After an early breakfast, embark on a day trip to Baratang Island. Explore the limestone caves and mud volcanoes, and enjoy the scenic beauty of the island. Return to Havelock in the evening for an overnight stay at the resort.

After breakfast, check out from the resort and take a ferry back to Port Blair. Visit the Chatham Saw Mill, one of the oldest sawmills in Asia, and explore the local markets for souvenirs. Overnight stay at the hotel in Port Blair.

After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to the airport for your departure flight. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the Andaman Islands and its natural beauty and heritage.
