Plan Your Trip

From permits to packing — we've got you covered for a seamless Arunachal experience.

ILP (Inner Line Permit)

Required for all visitors (Indian & foreign). Apply online at arunachalilp.com or through registered travel agents. Processing time: 3-7 days. Valid for 30 days.

Best Time to Visit

October-November (clear skies, festivals) and March-April (spring blooms, pleasant weather) are ideal. Avoid monsoon (June-Sept) due to landslides. Winter (Dec-Feb) offers snow in higher reaches.

Packing Checklist

Layers (0°C to 25°C), waterproof jacket, sturdy shoes, sunscreen, hat, power bank, basic medicines, and modest clothing for monastery visits. Carry cash — ATMs are limited outside towns.

Cultural Etiquette

Remove shoes before entering monasteries/homes, ask permission before photographing people, avoid public displays of affection, and respect sacred sites. Learn basic greetings in local languages.
