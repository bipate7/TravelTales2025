Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Andro Village & Homestay Check-in

Your journey begins as you arrive in Andro Village. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your cozy homestay, where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the stunning views of the village. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor traditional local dishes while relaxing at the homestay.

Day 2: Pottery Workshops & Cultural Exploration

Start your day with a hands-on pottery workshop where you can learn the traditional techniques from local artisans. In the afternoon, explore the vibrant culture of Andro Village by visiting local attractions and interacting with the community. Enjoy a guided tour that highlights the history and significance of the village's unique crafts.

Day 3: Scenic Beauty & Departure

After breakfast, take a leisurely walk through the picturesque landscapes surrounding Andro Village. Capture the beauty of the area before checking out from your homestay and heading to the nearest airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of the rich culture and stunning scenery of Andro Village.
