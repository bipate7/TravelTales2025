When to Visit Meghalaya?

Each season offers a unique experience — from misty monsoon magic to crisp winter clarity.

Spring (Mar–May)

15°C – 28°C
  • Pleasant weather for trekking
  • Rhododendrons in full bloom
  • Ideal for photography
  • Book early — peak season!
Plan My Spring Trip

Monsoon (Jun–Sep)

18°C – 24°C
  • Waterfalls at their fullest glory
  • Lush green landscapes
  • Trekking can be slippery
  • Limited visibility at viewpoints
Chase the Waterfalls

Autumn (Oct–Nov)

12°C – 22°C
  • Crystal-clear skies at Dawki
  • Low rain, high visibility
  • Best for root bridge treks
  • Harvest festivals begin
Book Autumn Adventure

Winter (Dec–Feb)

5°C – 18°C
  • Coolest & clearest weather
  • Panoramic views from Laitlum
  • Rare frost in highlands
  • Pack warm layers!
Winter Escape Package

Pro Tip: September & October offer the best balance — greenery remains, waterfalls are still active, and trails dry up just enough for safe trekking!
