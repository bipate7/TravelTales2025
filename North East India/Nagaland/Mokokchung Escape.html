Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Orientation

Arrive at Dimapur Airport, Nagaland. Check-in at the hotel and freshen up. In the evening, enjoy an orientation session about Mokokchung.

Day 2: Guided Tour of Mokokchung

Embark on a guided tour of Mokokchung, exploring its rich culture and beautiful landscapes. Learn about the Ao Naga traditions and enjoy photography opportunities.

Day 3: Nature Exploration

Spend the day exploring the natural beauty of Mokokchung, including local flora and fauna, and participate in cultural immersion activities.

Day 4: Shopping and Departure

Spend the morning shopping for traditional crafts and souvenirs. In the afternoon, check out from the hotel and head to the airport for your departure.
