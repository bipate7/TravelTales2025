Must-Visit Places in Haryana

From ancient historical sites to modern urban centers, Haryana offers a diverse range of attractions for every traveler.

Historical

Kurukshetra

Oct-Mar North Haryana

Known as the Land of the Bhagavad Gita, this historic city is where the epic Mahabharata war was fought. Visit Brahma Sarovar and Jyotisar for spiritual enlightenment.

Explore More
Historical

Panipat

Oct-Mar North Haryana

The City of Weavers and site of three historic battles that changed India's destiny. Explore Panipat Museum and Kabuli Bagh Mosque to witness its glorious past.

Explore More
Modern

Gurgaon

Oct-Mar South Haryana

Haryana's modern face with towering skyscrapers, shopping malls, and entertainment hubs. Don't miss Kingdom of Dreams and Sultanpur National Park.

Explore More
Cultural

Faridabad

Oct-Mar South Haryana

An industrial hub with cultural gems like Surajkund Crafts Mela and Badkhal Lake. Experience the perfect blend of industry and heritage.

Explore More
Nature

Panchkula

Oct-Mar North Haryana

Gateway to the Shivalik hills, known for Pinjore Gardens and Morni Hills. A perfect retreat for nature lovers and peace seekers.

Explore More
Wildlife

Sultanpur National Park

Oct-Mar South Haryana

A birdwatcher's paradise with over 250 species of resident and migratory birds. Enjoy tranquil walks amidst nature's beauty.

Explore More
Culinary

Murthal

Oct-Mar North Haryana

Famous for its roadside dhabas (eateries) that serve delicious North Indian food, particularly parathas.

Explore More
Historical

Pinjore Gardens

Oct-Apr North Haryana

A historic 17th-century Mughal garden, also known as Yadavindra Gardens. It features fountains, palaces, and well-maintained lawns.

Explore More
Historical

Fatehabad

Oct-Mar North Haryana

Known for its archaeological sites and historic significance, including the Fatehabad Fort and Bhima Devi Temple.

Explore More
Cultural

Rohtak

Oct-Mar North Haryana

A city with a rich history and culture, Rohtak is home to Tilyar Lake, Maharshi Dayanand University, and Bhindawas Bird Sanctuary.

Explore More
Historical

Rewari

Oct-Mar South Haryana

Known for its historic steam locomotive shed, Rewari is a must-visit for history enthusiasts. Other attractions include BMG Mall and Ghanteshwar Mandir.
