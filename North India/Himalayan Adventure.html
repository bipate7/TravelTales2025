Adventure Overview

The Himalayan Adventure tour takes you through the stunning landscapes of Uttarakhand, combining adrenaline-pumping activities with cultural immersion. This journey through the Garhwal Himalayas offers the perfect introduction to India's adventure capital, with professional guides ensuring your safety while maximizing the thrill.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Day 1: Arrival in Delhi - Transfer to Rishikesh

Your Himalayan adventure begins in Delhi, where our representative will greet you at the airport. After a brief orientation, we'll drive to Rishikesh (6-7 hours) through the scenic foothills of the Himalayas.

Highlights:

  • Evening Ganga Aarti ceremony at Triveni Ghat
  • Welcome dinner with local Garhwali cuisine
  • Briefing about the upcoming adventures

Overnight: Luxury riverside camp in Rishikesh

2

Day 2: Rishikesh - White Water Rafting & Yoga

Start your day with an exhilarating 16km rafting expedition on the Ganges (Grade III-IV rapids). After lunch, participate in a yoga session overlooking the river, followed by exploration of local temples and the iconic Laxman Jhula.

Activities:

  • Full-day rafting with professional guides
  • Hatha yoga session with certified instructor
  • Visit to Beatles Ashram and local markets

Overnight: Luxury riverside camp in Rishikesh

3

Day 3: Rishikesh to Auli - Journey to the Peaks

Scenic drive to Auli (8-9 hours) through picturesque mountain roads. Stop at Devprayag to witness the confluence of Alaknanda and Bhagirathi rivers forming the Ganges. Arrive in Auli to breathtaking views of Nanda Devi peak.

Highlights:

  • Visit to Devprayag sangam (river confluence)
  • Photo stops at scenic viewpoints
  • Evening acclimatization walk

Overnight: 4-star hotel in Auli with mountain views

4

Day 4: Auli - Trekking Expedition

Full-day guided trek through the alpine meadows of Auli with stunning Himalayan vistas. Choose between moderate (8km) or challenging (12km) routes based on your fitness level. Picnic lunch amidst nature with panoramic mountain views.

Activities:

  • Guided Himalayan trek with naturalist
  • Photography sessions at scenic viewpoints
  • Optional skiing lessons (seasonal)

Overnight: 4-star hotel in Auli

5

Day 5: Auli to Joshimath - Cable Car Experience

Morning cable car ride on Asia's longest (4km) and highest gondola, offering 360° views of Himalayan peaks. Explore Joshimath's ancient temples and vibrant markets. Evening cultural performance showcasing local Garhwali traditions.

Highlights:

  • Auli-Joshimath cable car ride
  • Visit to Narsingh Temple and Kalpavriksha
  • Local handicraft shopping

Overnight: Heritage property in Joshimath

6

Day 6: Joshimath - Optional Activities & Return to Rishikesh

Choose your adventure: Relax with yoga and meditation, or opt for an optional short trek to nearby viewpoints. After lunch, begin the return journey to Rishikesh with stops at Devprayag and other scenic spots.

Options:

  • Morning yoga session
  • Short trek to nearby viewpoints
  • Visit to local villages

Overnight: Riverside camp in Rishikesh

7

Day 7: Rishikesh to Delhi - Departure

After breakfast, drive back to Delhi (6-7 hours) with memories of your Himalayan adventure. Drop at airport or hotel for your onward journey.

Note: Flight bookings should be made for late evening or next day to account for possible road delays.

Essential Information

The Himalayan Adventure tour operates year-round, with different highlights each season:

Winter (Dec-Feb): Ideal for skiing in Auli with snow-covered landscapes. Day temperatures range from -5°C to 10°C. Pack heavy woolens and proper snow gear.

Spring (Mar-May): Perfect for trekking with pleasant 10-25°C temperatures and wildflowers in bloom. Clear mountain views.

Summer/Monsoon (Jun-Sep): Lush green landscapes with 15-30°C temperatures. Some rain but fewer crowds. Best for rafting with higher water levels.

Autumn (Oct-Nov): Crystal clear views of peaks with 5-20°C temperatures. Ideal for photography.

This adventure requires moderate to good physical fitness:

  • Comfortable walking 5-8 miles per day on uneven terrain
  • Ability to handle altitude up to 3000m
  • Basic swimming skills recommended for rafting
  • No prior trekking experience needed but helpful

We offer multiple difficulty levels for activities:

  • Rafting: Grade III-IV rapids (can opt for calmer sections)
  • Trekking: Choose between moderate (8km) or challenging (12km) routes
  • Altitude: Gradual ascent with acclimatization days

If you have health concerns, consult your doctor before booking. We can adjust itineraries for mobility limitations.

Essential Gear:

  • Sturdy hiking boots with ankle support
  • Waterproof jacket and pants
  • Layered clothing for variable temperatures
  • Warm hat and gloves (even in summer)
  • Swimwear for rafting

Equipment Provided:

  • Rafting gear (helmet, life jacket, paddle)
  • Trekking poles (on request)
  • Sleeping bags for camping (if needed)

Recommended Extras:

  • High SPF sunscreen and lip balm
  • Reusable water bottle (we provide filtered water)
  • Headlamp or flashlight
  • Basic first aid kit
  • Camera with extra batteries

This adventure can be enjoyed by families with children aged 12+ (younger children considered on case-by-case basis):

  • Special family rafting trips with calmer sections
  • Shorter, easier trekking options
  • Family rooms available at all accommodations
  • Child discounts available (12-18 years)

For younger adventurers (5-11 years), we recommend:

  • Modified rafting experiences
  • Gentle nature walks instead of treks
  • Cultural activities in Rishikesh and Joshimath

We can arrange babysitting services at hotels if parents want to participate in more challenging activities.

We offer flexible cancellation policies for this adventure:

  • Free cancellation up to 60 days before departure (full refund)
  • 30-59 days before departure: 75% refund
  • 15-29 days before departure: 50% refund
  • Less than 15 days: no refund, but we can reschedule (fees may apply)

For cancellations due to weather or safety concerns:

  • Full credit for future travel valid for 2 years
  • Alternative activities arranged when possible

Note: Travel insurance with adventure sports coverage is mandatory for this tour.
