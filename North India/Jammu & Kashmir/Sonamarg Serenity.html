Sonamarg Serenity Tour: Discover the Enchanting Beauty of Kashmir

Tour Details

Experience the breathtaking landscapes of Sonamarg with the Sonamarg Serenity Tour, designed to immerse you in the tranquil beauty of Kashmir. Known as the 'Meadow of Gold', Sonamarg is surrounded by snow-capped peaks and lush green meadows, making it a perfect getaway for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. This tour offers a unique opportunity to explore the untouched beauty of this region, often referred to as a hidden gem of Kashmir.


Tour Overview

Embark on a 4-day, 3-night adventure that showcases the stunning beauty of Sonamarg. From scenic drives through the mountains to leisurely walks in the meadows, this tour guarantees an unforgettable experience. Whether you seek adventure or relaxation, the Sonamarg Serenity Tour promises to leave you with cherished memories.

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar & Transfer to Sonamarg

Your journey begins as you arrive in Srinagar. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to Sonamarg, where you can check in to your accommodation and relax. Enjoy the stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys, and savor a delicious dinner featuring local cuisine.

Day 2: Thajiwas Glacier & Local Exploration

After breakfast, head to Thajiwas Glacier for a day of adventure. Engage in snow activities or simply enjoy the breathtaking views. In the afternoon, explore the local area, visiting nearby meadows and enjoying the serene environment.

Day 3: Nature Walks & Cultural Experience

Spend the day enjoying nature walks in the lush meadows of Sonamarg. Experience the local culture through traditional crafts and cuisine. In the evening, relax and take in the stunning sunset views over the mountains.

Day 4: Departure from Sonamarg

After breakfast, enjoy a leisurely morning before being transferred back to Srinagar for your departure. Take with you unforgettable memories of the beauty and tranquility of Sonamarg.
