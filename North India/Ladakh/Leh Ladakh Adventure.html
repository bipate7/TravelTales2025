Leh Ladakh: The Land of High Passes

Tour Details

Experience the rugged beauty of Leh and Ladakh with our specially curated adventure package. From the mesmerizing Pangong Lake to the high-altitude Khardung La pass, this tour offers an adrenaline-filled journey through the Himalayas.


Tour Overview

Embark on a 6-day, 5-night journey that takes you through the breathtaking landscapes of Ladakh. Visit ancient monasteries, ride camels in the Nubra Valley, and witness the magnetic hill phenomenon.

Tour Highlights

🗻 Pangong Lake: Visit the famous color-changing lake.

🏞 Nubra Valley: Experience the desert in the mountains and double-humped camels.

🚗 Khardung La: Drive through one of the highest motorable roads in the world.

🏛 Monasteries: Explore Thiksey and Hemis monasteries.

🌌 Magnetic Hill: Witness the gravity-defying hill.
