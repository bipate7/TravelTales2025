Tour Overview

The Rajasthan Heritage Tour is a curated journey through the royal state's most iconic cities. From the bustling streets of Jaipur to the serene lakes of Udaipur, and the imposing forts of Jodhpur, this tour offers a deep dive into the history, culture, and lifestyle of the Rajputs. Stay in heritage hotels, enjoy traditional performances, and create memories that last a lifetime.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur

Welcome to the Pink City! Our representative will meet you at the airport and transfer you to your heritage hotel. Relax and attend a brief orientation about the tour. In the evening, enjoy a welcome dinner with a traditional Rajasthani folk dance performance.

Overnight: Heritage hotel in Jaipur

2

Day 2: Jaipur - Amer Fort & City Exploration

Morning visit to the majestic Amer Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage site, followed by a photo stop at Hawa Mahal. Afternoon guided tour of City Palace, Jantar Mantar, and explore the local bazaars for textiles and handicrafts.

Overnight: Heritage hotel in Jaipur

3

Day 3: Jaipur to Jodhpur (via Ajmer-Pushkar)

En-route to Jodhpur, visit the holy city of Ajmer (Dargah Sharif) and the sacred Pushkar Lake with its Brahma Temple. Continue to Jodhpur, the Blue City, and check into your hotel. Evening leisure at the clock tower market.

Overnight: Heritage hotel in Jodhpur

4

Day 4: Jodhpur - Mehrangarh Fort & Blue City

Explore the imposing Mehrangarh Fort, one of India's largest forts. Descend to the blue Brahmin quarter for a unique perspective. Visit Jaswant Thada and enjoy a walk through the old city's narrow lanes. Evening cultural show at a local restaurant.

Overnight: Heritage hotel in Jodhpur

5

Day 5: Jodhpur to Udaipur (via Ranakpur)

Scenic drive to Udaipur, stopping at the exquisite Ranakpur Jain Temple, known for its intricate marble architecture. Arrive in Udaipur and enjoy a serene sunset boat ride on Lake Pichola.

Overnight: Palace hotel in Udaipur

6

Day 6: Udaipur - City Palace & Local Arts

Morning guided tour of the magnificent City Palace complex, followed by visits to Jagdish Temple and Sahelion-ki-Bari. Afternoon optional workshop on miniature painting or block printing. Evening at leisure by the lake.

Overnight: Palace hotel in Udaipur

7

Day 7: Udaipur to Jaisalmer (Flight + Desert Safari)

Morning transfer to Udaipur airport for a short flight to Jaisalmer. Arrive and check into your hotel. Later, proceed for a thrilling desert safari. Enjoy camel ride, sand dunes, and a cultural evening with folk music and dinner under the stars.

Overnight: Desert camp / Heritage hotel in Jaisalmer

8

Day 8: Jaisalmer - Golden Fort & Havelis

Explore the living Jaisalmer Fort, unique with its shops and residences. Visit the ornate Patwon-ki-Haveli and Salim Singh-ki-Haveli. Free time for shopping local handicrafts. Evening farewell dinner at a rooftop restaurant overlooking the fort.

Overnight: Heritage hotel in Jaisalmer

9

Day 9: Jaisalmer to Jaipur/Delhi - Departure

After breakfast, transfer to Jaisalmer airport for your flight to Jaipur or Delhi to connect with your international flight. Tour concludes with unforgettable memories of Rajasthan's royalty.

Note: Flight bookings should be made for late afternoon/evening.

Essential Information

The ideal time for this tour is from October to March when the weather is pleasant for sightseeing. Winters can be chilly (5-10°C) in the mornings and evenings, while days are sunny and warm. The desert safari is most enjoyable during this period. For those who prefer fewer crowds and don't mind heat, April-June offers lower prices but temperatures can soar above 40°C.

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Light cotton clothes for day, warm layers for evenings (winter)
  • Sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat
  • Scarf/shawl (for visiting temples)
  • Camera with extra batteries
  • Reusable water bottle
  • Basic medications and first-aid

Rajasthan is generally safe for travelers. We recommend:

  • Drinking only bottled or filtered water
  • Being cautious with street food
  • Carrying hand sanitizer
  • Travel insurance covering medical emergencies
  • Staying hydrated, especially in summer

Our guides are first-aid trained and we have 24/7 support.
