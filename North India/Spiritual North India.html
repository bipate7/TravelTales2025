Spiritual Overview

This 9-day pilgrimage traces the holiest cities along the Ganges. From the ancient ghats of Varanasi to the serene Himalayas in Rishikesh, you'll be guided by local priests and yoga masters, ensuring a journey that nourishes the soul.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Day 1: Arrival in Varanasi

Meet & greet at airport, transfer to hotel. Evening free for leisure. At sunset, witness the spectacular Ganga Aarti at Dashashwamedh Ghat – a ceremony of fire, incense and mantras.

Overnight: Heritage hotel near the Ghats

2

Day 2: Varanasi – Sunrise Boat & Sarnath

Early morning boat ride on the Ganges, see the ghats come alive. Visit Kashi Vishwanath Temple. Afternoon excursion to Sarnath, where Buddha gave his first sermon.

Overnight: Varanasi

3

Day 3: Varanasi – Bodhgaya (6 hrs drive)

Drive to Bodhgaya. Evening meditation at the Mahabodhi Temple complex, under the Bodhi tree.

Overnight: Bodhgaya

4

Day 4: Bodhgaya – Full Day Exploration

Visit Mahabodhi Stupa, Great Buddha Statue, and various international monasteries. Optional: meditation session with monks.

Overnight: Bodhgaya

5

Day 5: Bodhgaya – Prayagraj (Allahabad) (5 hrs)

Drive to Prayagraj. Evening Aarti at Sangam – the sacred confluence. Optional holy dip.

Overnight: Prayagraj

6

Day 6: Prayagraj – Varanasi – flight to Haridwar

Morning drive back to Varanasi. Fly to Dehradun, then drive to Haridwar (1 hr). Evening Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri.

Overnight: Haridwar

7

Day 7: Haridwar – Rishikesh (1 hr)

Visit ashrams, Laxman Jhula, Ram Jhula. Evening yoga session by the Ganges.

Overnight: Rishikesh

8

Day 8: Rishikesh – Meditation & Optional Trek

Full day for personal reflection. Join a meditation workshop or trek to Neer Garh waterfall.

Overnight: Rishikesh

9

Day 9: Rishikesh – Delhi Departure

Drive to Delhi (5-6 hrs) for your onward flight. Tour ends with divine memories.

Note: Book flights after 8:00 PM.
