Tour Plans

Day 1-3: Yamunotri

Drive from Haridwar to Barkot. Trek to Yamunotri temple for darshan and return. Visit Surya Kund and Divya Shila.

Day 4-5: Gangotri

Drive to Uttarkashi. Visit Gangotri temple. Darshan of Bhagirathi river and Gauri Kund.

Day 6-8: Kedarnath

Drive to Guptkashi. Trek to Kedarnath (19km). Darshan of Lord Shiva. Stay overnight or return.

Day 9-10: Badrinath

Drive to Badrinath via Joshimath. Darshan of Lord Vishnu. Visit Mana Village, Vyas Gufa, and Bhim Pul.

Day 11-12: Rishikesh & Departure

Drive back to Rishikesh. Visit Ram Jhula and Laxman Jhula. Drop at Haridwar/Dehradun/Delhi for departure.
