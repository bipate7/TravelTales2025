Tour Details

Discover the spiritual essence of Haridwar with our special package. Located at the foothills of the Himalayas, Haridwar is one of the seven holiest places in Hinduism. This tour includes accommodation, guided tours, and a chance to immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of this sacred city.


Tour Overview

Embark on a 4-day, 3-night adventure that takes you through the best that Haridwar has to offer. From visiting ancient temples to experiencing the Ganga Aarti, this tour ensures a truly enriching experience. Whether you seek spiritual enlightenment or wish to explore the local culture, the Haridwar Heritage Tour promises to be a journey you’ll cherish forever.

Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Haridwar

Your journey begins as you arrive in the holy city of Haridwar. Upon arrival, you will check into your hotel and have some time to relax. In the evening, experience the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri, a spiritual ceremony that will leave you in awe.

Day 2: Temple Tours

Start your day with a visit to the famous Mansa Devi Temple, located on a hilltop. You can take a cable car ride to reach the temple. Afterward, visit the Chandi Devi Temple and explore the local markets for traditional handicrafts.

Day 3: Cultural Immersion

Delve into the local culture by participating in rituals and learning about the traditions of Haridwar. Visit the local ashrams and enjoy a cooking class to learn how to prepare authentic Indian dishes.

Day 4: Departure

After breakfast, enjoy some leisure time before checking out. You can visit any local attractions you may have missed before departing for your onward journey.
