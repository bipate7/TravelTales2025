Tour Details

Experience the thrill of Rishikesh with the Rishikesh Adventure Tour, a carefully designed journey that combines adventure, nature, and cultural exploration. Embark on a 5-day, 4-night adventure that takes you through the best of Rishikesh and its surrounding villages. Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Rishikesh & Camp Stay

Your adventure begins as you arrive in Rishikesh. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your deluxe camp where you'll check in and unwind. Enjoy the serene atmosphere as you take in the stunning views of the Ganges and surrounding hills. A leisurely evening awaits you, where you can savor traditional local dishes while relaxing at the camp.

Day 2: Rafting on the Ganges

Start your day with an exhilarating rafting experience on the Ganges. Navigate through thrilling rapids and enjoy the breathtaking scenery along the river. After rafting, you can relax at the camp or explore the local area.

Day 3: Bungee Jumping & Local Exploration

For the thrill-seekers, today includes an optional bungee jumping experience from one of the highest points in India. Afterward, explore the local markets and visit iconic landmarks like the Triveni Ghat and Parmarth Niketan Ashram.

Day 4: Trekking and Yoga

Embark on a scenic trek to nearby villages, where you can experience the local culture and hospitality. In the evening, participate in a rejuvenating yoga and meditation session by the river, surrounded by nature.

Day 5: Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning by the Ganges before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Rishikesh and its stunning landscapes.
