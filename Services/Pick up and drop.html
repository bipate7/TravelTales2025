Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and booking process.

How do I book a ride?

You can book a ride through our website, mobile app, or by calling our customer service. Online bookings are available 24/7 and provide instant confirmation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash payments to the driver.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking through the app or website up to 1 hour before the scheduled pickup time. Cancellation fees may apply depending on timing.

Are your drivers licensed and insured?

All our drivers are professionally licensed, fully insured, and undergo rigorous background checks and training to ensure your safety and comfort.

Do you provide child seats?

Yes, we offer child seats upon request at an additional fee. Please specify your needs when booking to ensure availability.
