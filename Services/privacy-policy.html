Privacy Policy

Your trust is our compass. Here’s how we protect your journey — and your data.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Travel Tales ("we", "us", "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.traveltales.com, use our app, or share your travel stories with us.

This policy applies to all users, including contributors, subscribers, and visitors.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect:

  • Identity Data: Name, email address, profile picture (if uploaded)
  • Contact Data: Email, phone number (if provided)
  • Content Data: Travel stories, photos, videos, location tags, and comments you submit
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device ID, cookies, usage data

2.2 How We Collect It

  • Directly from you (forms, uploads, communications)
  • Automatically via cookies and analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • From third parties (e.g., social login via Google or Facebook — with your consent)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our platform and services
  • Publish and curate travel stories (with your permission)
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send newsletters and updates (you may opt out anytime)
  • Analyze usage trends to enhance user experience
  • Prevent fraud, spam, and illegal activities

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Hosting, email, analytics (e.g., AWS, SendGrid, Google Analytics)
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or asset sale (users will be notified)

All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption (HTTPS)
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and employee training
  • Secure storage (encrypted at rest)

⚠️ No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We strive to protect, but cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location (e.g., under GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability (receive a copy in machine-readable format)
  • Withdraw consent (without affecting prior processing)

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@traveltales.com.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember preferences (e.g., language, theme)
  • Analyze site traffic and user behavior
  • Enable social sharing features

You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may limit site functionality.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The updated version will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
