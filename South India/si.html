off the map: hidden south

Places you won't find in guidebooks — whispered about by locals, untouched by crowds.

Kolli Hills

Misty peaks, ancient medicinal plants, and a 70‑hairpin road. Minimal tourism, maximal soul.

Chandragiri, Kerala

A backwater untouched by commercial houseboats — paddle through silent canals past toddy‑tappers.

Belavadi

Hoysala temple of rare beauty, always empty. The sculptures whisper stories of a forgotten empire.

Ranganathittu

Migratory birds by the thousand — boat ride among painted storks, ibis, and cormorants.
