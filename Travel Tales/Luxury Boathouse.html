Redefining Backwater Experiences

Solar-Powered Luxury

The Kerala Tourism Development Corporation has introduced 15 new premium houseboats with solar power, private chefs, and curated cultural experiences, taking the backwater tourism in "God's Own Country" to a whole new level of luxury and sustainability.

These state-of-the-art houseboats feature contemporary Keralite architecture combined with modern amenities. Each boat is equipped with solar panels that provide up to 80% of its energy needs, significantly reducing the environmental impact of tourism in the fragile backwater ecosystem.

The interiors showcase exquisite craftsmanship with traditional materials like rosewood, coir, and bamboo. The houseboats offer spacious living areas, luxurious bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms, and open decks perfect for enjoying the serene landscape.

Village Life

Immersive Backwater Journey

The houseboats cruise through the picturesque backwaters of Alleppey, offering guests an intimate view of rural Kerala life. Passengers can watch farmers tending to their paddy fields, children heading to school in traditional boats, and local artisans at work.

  • Guided village tours and interactions with local communities
  • Traditional fishing experiences with local fishermen
  • Visit to coir-making units and other cottage industries
  • Bird watching in the rich ecosystem of the backwaters
  • Sunset views from the open deck with refreshments
  • Traditional Kerala martial arts and dance demonstrations

These new additions are part of Kerala's initiative to promote sustainable tourism that benefits local communities while preserving the natural and cultural heritage of the region. The houseboats are operated by trained local staff, creating employment opportunities in the area.
