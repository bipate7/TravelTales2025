Our Story, Your Journey

For over a decade, we've been crafting unforgettable travel experiences that connect people with the world's most extraordinary places.

Where Passion Meets Purpose

TravelTales was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to transform how people experience the world. Founded in 2012 by a group of passionate travelers and industry veterans, we set out to create a travel company that prioritizes authentic experiences over mass tourism.

What started as a small team in Nagpur has grown into a nationwide network of travel experts, local guides, and partners who share our vision of responsible, immersive travel. Today, we've helped over 50,000 travelers create memories that last a lifetime.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make and every journey we create

Authentic Connection

We believe travel is about meaningful connections—with people, places, and cultures. We design experiences that foster genuine understanding and appreciation.

Sustainable Travel

Our commitment to responsible tourism means supporting local communities, protecting natural environments, and preserving cultural heritage for future generations.

Exceptional Quality

From handpicked accommodations to expert local guides, we maintain the highest standards in every aspect of your journey. Your experience is our reputation.

Personalized Service

We understand that every traveler is unique. Our dedicated travel consultants work closely with you to create itineraries that match your interests, pace, and style.

Trust & Safety

Your peace of mind is our priority. We provide 24/7 support, comprehensive insurance options, and partnerships with trusted local operators worldwide.

Innovation & Curiosity

We constantly explore new destinations, unique experiences, and innovative technologies to make your journey more seamless, informative, and inspiring.
