Top Attractions

Daman Beaches

Best time: Oct-Mar

Jampore and Devka beaches offer golden sands, water sports, and stunning sunsets along the Arabian Sea.

Explore more

Diu Fort

Best time: Oct-Mar

A magnificent Portuguese fort with panoramic sea views, ancient cannons, and fascinating history.

Explore more

Nagoa Beach

Best time: Oct-Mar

Horseshoe-shaped beach known for its palm-fringed coastline and water sports activities.

Explore more

St. Paul's Church

Best time: Oct-Mar

A beautiful 17th-century Portuguese church with exquisite wood carvings and Baroque architecture.

Explore more

Gangeshwar Temple

Best time: Oct-Mar

Ancient Shiva temple with five lingams naturally carved by sea waves, located near the sea.

Explore more

Naida Caves

Best time: Oct-Mar

Network of caves with fascinating rock formations and natural skylights created by erosion.

Explore more
