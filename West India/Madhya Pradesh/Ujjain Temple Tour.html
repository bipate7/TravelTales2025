Tour Plans

Day 1: Arrival in Ujjain & Temple Visit

Your journey begins as you arrive in Ujjain. Upon arrival, you will be transferred to your hotel. After checking in, visit the iconic Mahakaleshwar Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, and participate in the evening Aarti ceremony. Enjoy a leisurely evening exploring the local markets.

Day 2: Explore Ujjain's Temples

Start your day with a visit to Harsiddhi Temple and Kal Bhairav Temple. Learn about the rich history and architecture of these ancient temples. In the afternoon, take a boat ride on the Shipra River and enjoy the scenic views of the ghats.

Day 3: Cultural Immersion & Local Cuisine

Delve deeper into the culture of Ujjain as you explore its vibrant markets. Visit local artisans and shop for traditional handicrafts. In the evening, enjoy a cooking class where you will learn to prepare authentic Ujjain cuisine.

Day 4: Departure

After breakfast, enjoy a peaceful morning before being transferred to the airport for your departure. Carry with you unforgettable memories of Ujjain and its spiritual heritage.
